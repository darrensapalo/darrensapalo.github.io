How to Install and Use Podcasts

I know some of you might encounter some problems installing and using Podcasts, so I added pictures here to make it simpler.

  1. Open the Google Playstore App.
  2. At the top is a white search bar; type "Podcast" and search.
  3. Download Podcast & Radio Addict, or you can choose whatever Podcast player to use. Think of it like a music player, but you can download music.
  4. Once downloaded, Allow any permissions required for the Podcast & Radio Addict. It will ask for permissions to access your files so that it can save the Podcasts in your phone.
  5. When you open the app, you can read some explanation of how to begin using Podcasts.
  6. Search whatever topic you're interested in (i.e. Computer Science).
  7. Look at all the search results and find something you want to try out.
  8. Once you've selected what to try, subscribe to the Podcast you selected. That way you'll know when they publish new episodes.
  9. Pick one of the earliest episodes to begin studying (It should be at the bottom of the list)!
  10. There's three vertical dots on the side of the episode. Tap it to opens up the options.
  11. Download the episode to listen to it even offline!

    This is what it looks like when it's dowloading.

  12. Once you're done downloading and listening to the episode, remember to delete the episode so you don't run out of space on your phone!
